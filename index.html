About Me

I am a 3D Artist specialized in animation and visual storytelling. Over the years, I have explored different aspects of 3D - from modeling and texturing to rigging and animation - but what truly defines my work is the ability to give life and motion to digital creations.

I strive to make my characters expressive, my worlds immersive, and my animations dynamic and fluid. This balance of creativity and precision allows me to adapt to different styles, from stylized fantasy to realistic cinematic visuals. My ultimate goal is always the same: to create 3D art that not only looks impressive but also feels alive and tells a story.





3D Animation

Creating story.

For me, animation is the heartbeat of 3D. It’s the moment when a still model suddenly moves, breathes, and feels alive. I love shaping that transformation - from the tiniest facial expression to a full character performance. My process is about finding the right rhythm and emotion in every movement. I keep my poses strong, my motion fluid, and my characters believable, so they don’t just look good - they feel real. Whether it’s for games, film, or short projects, my goal is simple: to create animations that people connect with.
